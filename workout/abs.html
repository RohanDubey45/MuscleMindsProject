Abs Workout

Crunches

A classic abs exercise that targets the upper body and core.

No. of Reputations:

Total 3 sets of minimum 12 - 14 reps each

How to Perform:

Follow these steps to perform flat bench-press correctly:

  1. Lie flat on bench, feet on ground. Grip barbell slightly wider than shoulders.
  2. Lower barbell to chest, elbows at 90 degrees. Keep back pressed against the bench.
  3. Push barbell up, fully extending arms. Engage chest muscles throughout.

A classic abs exercise that targets the upper body and core.

No. of Reputations:

Total 3 sets of minimum 12 - 14 reps each

How to Perform:

Follow these steps to perform peck-deck fly correctly:

  1. Adjust the seat and handles on the pec deck machine.Sit with your back flat against the pad.
  2. Grasp the handles with your elbows bent at 90 degrees.Bring the handles together in front of your chest.
  3. Keep a slight bend in your elbows during the movement.Open your arms back to the starting position.Maintain control and focus on the chest contraction.

Side Plank

A classic abs exercise that targets the upper body and core.

No. of Reputations

Total 3 sets of minimum 12 - 14 reps each

How to Perform:

Follow these steps to perform chest dips correctly:

  1. Position yourself between parallel bars or on a dip station.Grip each bar with your palms facing inward.
  2. Extend your arms fully and lift your body off the ground.Lean slightly forward to target the chest muscles.
  3. Keep your chest up and maintain a slight forward lean.Push through your palms to raise your body back up.Straighten your arms fully at the top of the movement.
